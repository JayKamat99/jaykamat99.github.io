CloSE: A Compact Shape- and Orientation-Agnostic Cloth State Representatione

submitted to IROS 2025

1Institut de Robòtica i Informàtica Industrial, CSIC-UPC

Abstract

Cloth manipulation is a difficult problem mainly because of the non-rigid nature of cloth, which makes a good representation of deformation essential. We present a new representation for the deformation-state of clothes. First, we propose the dGLI disk representation, based on topological indices computed for segments on the edges of the cloth mesh border that are arranged on a circular grid, similarly as done in literature with dGLI matrices. The heat-map of the dGLI disk uncovers patterns that correspond to features of the cloth state, like the corner positions and the fold locations. These patterns are consistent for different shapes, sizes and cloth positions. We then present a method to abstract these important features from the dGLI disk into a circle, calling it the Cloth StatE representation (CloSE). This representation is compact, continuous, and general for different shapes. Finally, we show the strengths of this representation in two relevant applications: semantic labeling and high- and low-level planning.
